Steps to sign in and registration.

1. Go to home page.

2. Click on sign up button on top.

3. If you already have an account then add username and password on the visible page and click on login button.

4. Else if you are new to Mera kisan then click on sign up button and it will take you to a new page.

5. Enter first name, last name, email, username, phone no, and password and then click on sign up.

6. Once you are signed up follow step 3.

7. Congratulations you are successfully signed in to Mera kisan.

Steps to add your product

1. Go to home page.

2. Click on shop now button on top right corner.

3. To proceed further follow step 3 or 4 and 5 from steps to sign in and registration.

4. If you are on add product page, then enter username, name of your product, category, quantity, your city name, section and price of your product.

5. Then click on enter button to add your product.

6. Congratulations your product has been added to Mera kisan.
